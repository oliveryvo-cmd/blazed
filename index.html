// WHAT IS B.L.A.Z.E.D?

MORE THAN A BOX.

B.L.A.Z.E.D is a surprise joy box company built entirely for disabled kids. Every box is packed by hand, filled with real toys and fun stuff, and comes with a handwritten note from Oliver himself. It's not charity. It's love in a box.

Disabled kids sometimes feel like the world has forgotten about them. They feel different. Left out. Like joy is for other people. B.L.A.Z.E.D exists to prove them wrong.

Every single box is a surprise — packed with toys, slime, Mini Brands, Nee Doh, Labubu figures, and more. You never know exactly what's inside until you open it. That's the whole point. Pure, unexpected joy.

And at the bottom of every box — a handwritten note from Oliver. Not a printed card. A real note, written by hand, just for that kid.
